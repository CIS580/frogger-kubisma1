Play Frogger

Instructions

Use your left, up and right arrow key to run the frog across the
road and the river. Stay aware of cars, they can hit you. Jump on logs
in order not to be drown.

Somehow it works. I spent so much time developing this game (approx. 30 hours)
and still I'm not satisfied with the game behaviour. I think it's too
much for one-week assignment...
